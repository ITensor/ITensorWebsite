Official Codes

These are packages based on ITensor developed partly or in full by the ITensor developers. We aim to support these codes at the same level as the core ITensor library itself.


[Giacomo Torlai, Matt Fishman]

PastaQ is a Julia package for working with quantum hardware and quantum circuits. It includes tools for quantum state and process tomography, optimization of quantum circuits, and simulations of quantum circuits.


TDVP [Mingru Yang]

The official TDVP implementation for the C++ version of ITensor.


Community Codes

These are codes based on ITensor by other members of our community. While we cannot guarantee or provide technical support for these codes, we still welcome discussion of them in our support forum and encourage users to try out and contribute to these community codes.
